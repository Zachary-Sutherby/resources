Description:
This GeoNet article descripts the workflow to use a Python script and windows task scheduler to connect to ArcGIS Online or Portal, download the survey Form item, extract the zip file to a folder. It then copies the updated media folder files from a known location into the extracted folders, zip the form item back up again and upload it to ArcGIS Online or Portal.

The field workers will need to delete the survey from their gallery in the field app on the device, re-download the survey from the Download Surveys menu, open the survey and they will have access to the updated survey and associated media files.
